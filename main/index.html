Posterstock

A personalized gallery for movies and series posters—simple, stylish, and clutter-free. Added items are displayed as eye-catching posters, with the option to invest in exclusive NFT artworks by top artists, ready for collection or trade.

DEX Investment Token POSTERSTOCK

Revenue Sharing
Token holders are entitled to receive a portion of Posterstock's revenue, creating a direct connection between the platform's success and investor returns.

Transparency and Analytics
Holders gain access to comprehensive platform statistics and analytics, enabling them to monitor the service's performance metrics, growth indicators, and revenue data.

Governance Rights
The token grants voting privileges on selected platform decisions, allowing holders to actively participate in shaping Posterstock's development and future direction.

CEX Trading Token PSTC

Exchange Trading
The token is designed for trading on centralized cryptocurrency exchanges, providing liquidity and market accessibility to users worldwide.

Community Airdrop Program
Active platform users will receive guaranteed airdrops based on their genuine platform engagement and poster purchasing history. This reward system is designed exclusively for real users, with robust measures to prevent bot participation.

Utility Features
PSTC tokens can be used to purchase exclusive posters within the Posterstock platform. Selected premium posters are available only for PSTC token holders, creating additional utility value for the token.
