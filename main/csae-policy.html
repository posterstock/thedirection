CSAE Compliance Statement

This CSAE Compliance Statement outlines how Posterstock application ("we," "our," or "us") ensures complete compliance with Google Play requirements regarding the prevention of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) materials.

1. Technical Prevention Measures

Posterstock implements a closed content system that completely eliminates the possibility of user-generated content uploads. All content presented in the application is pre-installed and thoroughly verified.

2. Control and Moderation Systems

2.1. No User Content Upload: The application has no functionality for uploading user-generated content.

2.2. No File Sharing: There are no file-sharing systems between users.

2.3. No User Profiles: No functionality for creating user profiles.

2.4. No Communication Features: No chat systems or communication features between users.

3. Commitments

We confirm that our application:

3.1. Does not contain any CSAE-related materials.

3.2. Has no technical capabilities for distributing such materials.

3.3. Features an architecture that prevents its use for distributing prohibited content.

4. Monitoring and Compliance

We maintain strict oversight of our application's content and regularly review our security measures to ensure ongoing compliance with CSAE prevention requirements.

6. Updates to This Statement

We reserve the right to update this compliance statement as necessary to maintain alignment with Google Play requirements and our commitment to preventing CSAE content.
